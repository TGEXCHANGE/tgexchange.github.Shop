How to Sell Your Telegram Group

  1. You must be the owner (creator) of the group.
  2. The group must be a Private Group.
  3. "Chat History For New Members" option must be visible.
  4. The creation date of the group must be in the price list.
  5. Provide the group's invite link for us to join.
  6. Transfer the group ownership to our representative.
  7. We will review the groups for compliance.
  8. After approval, you will receive your payment (USDT).
